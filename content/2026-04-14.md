## 🔗 表达 / Chunking Expressions

**stop fighting** （停止战斗）
A phrase used to indicate the cessation of armed conflict.
- The two countries decided to stop fighting for a period of time.
- After months of war, the rebels agreed to stop fighting.

**changed his mind** （改变了主意）
Used when someone decides to do something different from what they had decided before. It's a common idiom.
- I was going to go to the party, but I changed my mind at the last minute.
- She wanted to buy the red dress, but then she changed her mind and chose the blue one.

**future of NATO** （北约的未来）
Refers to the plans, prospects, or continuing existence of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization.
- Leaders discussed the future of NATO and its role in global security.
- The article explores different opinions about the future of NATO in the current geopolitical climate.

**worried about** （担心）
Expresses concern or anxiety regarding a particular person, situation, or outcome.
- Many citizens are worried about the rising unemployment rate.
- She is worried about her exams next week.
