The Sagrada Família is a big church in Spain. It is very old and very tall. It is now the tallest church in the world. Many people visit the church every day.

They like the beautiful colors and the high towers. It is a very famous place for tourists from every country.

But there is a problem. The church needs more space for new stairs. To make this space, the city wants to take away some houses. The people who live there are worried. They love their homes. They do not want to move. They want to stay in their neighborhood with their friends.

Now, the city and the people are talking about the plan. It is a big change for the city of Barcelona. Some people want the tall church. Some people want the old houses. We do not know what will happen next. But we hope that every person can be happy and safe in their home.

## 🔗 表达 / Chunking Expressions

**very old and very tall** （非常古老且非常高）
Used to describe something that has existed for a long time and has great height. Common in descriptive language.
- The ancient castle was very old and very tall, dominating the landscape.
- She inherited a very old and very tall grandfather clock from her grandmother.

**take away** （拿走，移走）
To remove something from a place or person. Can be literal or figurative.
- The landlord decided to take away the old furniture.
- The new policy will take away some of our freedoms.

**talking about** （谈论）
Engaging in conversation or discussion about a particular subject. Very common in everyday communication.
- They spent the evening talking about their future plans.
- The news channels are talking about the recent election results.

**will happen next** （接下来会发生什么）
Used to express uncertainty about future events. Common when discussing ongoing situations or predictions.
- Nobody knows what will happen next in this mysterious story.
- After the merger, we are all wondering what will happen next.
