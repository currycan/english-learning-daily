## 🔗 表达 / Chunking Expressions

**power plant** （发电厂）
Refers to a facility that generates electric power. Common in news and technical contexts.
- The city is planning to build a new solar power plant.
- Many jobs are available at the local power plant.

**clean the area** （清理该区域）
To remove dirt, waste, or harmful substances from a specific location. Used in contexts of cleanup operations or maintenance.
- The volunteers are helping to clean the area after the festival.
- It took days to clean the area affected by the oil spill.

**sad day** （悲伤的一天）
A day that is marked by unhappiness, grief, or remembrance of something negative. Used in both personal and public contexts.
- It was a sad day for the community when the old library closed.
- The anniversary of the event is a sad day for many families.

**stop near** （靠近停止）
To cease movement or activity in the vicinity of a particular place. Used when discussing safety, traffic, or military actions.
- Please stop near the entrance so we can meet.
- The authorities have ordered all aircraft to stop near the restricted zone.
