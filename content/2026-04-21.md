## 🔗 表达 / Chunking Expressions

**stay safe** （保持安全）
To remain protected from danger or harm. Used when talking about personal safety or the security of a place or group.
- The hikers were told to stay safe in the mountains.
- It's important for children to learn how to stay safe online.

**in the air** （在空中）
Describes something that is flying or floating in the sky. Often used for aircraft or birds.
- The planes were in the air for over two hours.
- We saw balloons in the air at the festival.

**working with** （与...合作）
Collaborating or cooperating with someone or something to achieve a common goal.
- The company is working with universities on new research.
- She enjoys working with her colleagues on this project.

**ready for** （准备好迎接/应对）
Prepared for a particular situation or event. Indicates a state of readiness.
- We need to be ready for the exam next week.
- Is everyone ready for the presentation?
