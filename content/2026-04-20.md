## 🔗 表达 / Chunking Expressions

**fly together** （一起飞行）
To operate in the air at the same time and in the same space. Used for aircraft.
- The new air traffic control system allows drones and helicopters to fly together.
- Birds often fly together in large groups for protection.

**make them better** （使它们更好）
To improve something, especially in order to achieve a better result or performance.
- The company is constantly testing new features to make their products better.
- Students should review their mistakes to make their next exam performance better.

**moving things** （移动东西）
The act of transporting objects from one location to another.
- Delivery services are very efficient at moving things to people's homes.
- The construction workers are busy moving things to the new building site.

**in our sky** （在我们的天空中）
Refers to the area above the Earth that is visible from the ground, now increasingly populated by various aerial vehicles.
- With more satellites being launched, there's a lot of activity in our sky.
- The artist painted a beautiful sunset in our sky.
