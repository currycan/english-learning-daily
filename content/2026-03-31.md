## 📖 文章 / Article

30-03-2026 15:00

Plastic is in our clothes, our food, and now in our bodies. We breathe it in the air and drink it in water. This is a big problem for us.

These small pieces of plastic stay in our skin and our blood. They can make our bodies feel sick and tired. Our body cannot destroy them. We need to use less plastic every day. Do not use plastic bottles or plastic boxes for food. Use glass, wood, or metal in your kitchen. This is better for your health.

We also need official rules to stop making so much plastic. If we all work together, we can make the world clean and stay healthy.

Difficult words: breathe (to take air into your lungs and let it out again), destroy (to damage something badly), health (how well or sick you feel).

## 🔗 表达 / Chunking Expressions

**breathe it in** （吸入（它））
Used to describe inhaling something, often a substance in the air.
- We breathe it in the air and drink it in water.
- People often breathe it in when they are near the factory.

**make our bodies feel sick** （使我们的身体感到不适）
Describes the effect of something on a person's physical well-being, causing illness or discomfort.
- These small pieces of plastic stay in our skin and our blood. They can make our bodies feel sick and tired.
- Eating too much unhealthy food can make your body feel sick.

**work together** （一起工作，合作）
To collaborate or cooperate with others to achieve a common goal. This is a common and positive expression.
- If we all work together, we can make the world clean and stay healthy.
- The students decided to work together on the group project.

**better for your health** （对你的健康更好）
Indicates a positive impact on one's physical well-being. Often used to give advice.
- Use glass, wood, or metal in your kitchen. This is better for your health.
- Drinking more water is generally better for your health.
