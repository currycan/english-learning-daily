## 🔗 表达 / Chunking Expressions

**fly together** （一起飞行）
Used to describe when aircraft, like drones and planes, operate in the same airspace at the same time. It can be used in both literal and figurative senses, but in this article, it's literal.
- In the future, with new technology, it might be possible for drones and helicopters to fly together over cities.
- The simulation showed that the two types of aircraft could safely fly together under controlled conditions.

**new system** （新系统）
Refers to a recently developed or implemented set of methods, rules, or equipment designed to achieve a particular goal.
- The company implemented a new system for managing customer complaints.
- Scientists are developing a new system to detect early signs of the disease.

**make them better** （使它们更好）
This phrase means to improve something, to increase its quality, performance, or usefulness. It is often used when talking about fixing problems or developing products.
- By studying the mistakes, engineers can make the next version of the robot much better.
- The feedback from users helped the developers make the app better.

**keep everyone safe** （保护大家安全）
This phrase emphasizes the importance of ensuring that all people in a particular situation or place are protected from harm or danger. It highlights a collective responsibility for safety.
- The new traffic rules are designed to keep everyone safe on the roads.
- It is the responsibility of the organizers to keep everyone safe during the festival.
