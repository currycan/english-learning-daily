## 📖 文章 / Article

10-04-2026 15:00

The shoe business is changing. Nike, the biggest shoe brand, is having a hard time.

For a few years, Nike didn’t create many new styles, so shoppers started buying shoes from other brands like Hoka and On. Now, Nike has a new boss who wants to make the brand special again.

At the same time, Crocs are popular again. Many people thought they were ugly, but nowadays, you can decorate them with small charms. This helped them sell billions of dollars of shoes. A Swiss brand called On is also becoming a leader. Their shoes look very different and are popular with people who run. On is growing much faster than older brands.

But there is a risk for the future. Many shoes come from Vietnam, and new taxes may make the price of shoes go up. Brands must find ways to stay popular even when prices are rising.

Difficult words: brand (the name and image of a company), ugly (not pleasant to look at, not beautiful), charm (a small, decorative object).

## 🔗 表达 / Chunking Expressions

**having a hard time** （处境困难）
This phrase means that a person or company is experiencing difficulties and struggles.
- The small business is having a hard time competing with larger stores.
- After the accident, she was having a hard time adjusting to her new life.

**make the brand special again** （让品牌再次变得特别）
This expression refers to efforts to restore the unique appeal and value of a company's brand.
- The new marketing campaign aims to make the brand special again for younger customers.
- By focusing on quality and innovation, the chef tried to make the restaurant special again.

**popular with** （受...欢迎）
This is used to say that something or someone is liked by a particular group of people.
- These casual sneakers are very popular with students.
- Her latest book is popular with readers of all ages.

**stay popular** （保持受欢迎）
This means to continue to be liked or admired by many people over a period of time.
- It's challenging for artists to stay popular as trends change quickly.
- The company needs to innovate to stay popular in the market.
