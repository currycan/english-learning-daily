## 🔗 表达 / Chunking Expressions

**in public** （公开地）
When something is done or said where many people can see or hear it.
- She rarely speaks in public.
- The company announced the news in public yesterday.

**talk about** （谈论）
To discuss or mention a particular subject.
- We need to talk about the project deadline.
- He doesn't like to talk about his past.

**in front of** （在...前面）
Physically located before someone or something, or in a public setting before an audience.
- The children performed a play in front of their parents.
- She felt nervous standing in front of the large crowd.

**surprised by** （对...感到惊讶）
Feeling or showing surprise because something unexpected happened.
- We were all surprised by the sudden change in weather.
- He was surprised by the gift he received.
