## 🔗 表达 / Chunking Expressions

**tall buildings** （高楼大厦）
Refers to very high structures, common in modern cities. Used in descriptive contexts.
- New York City is famous for its many tall buildings.
- The skyline was dominated by tall buildings.

**big change** （巨大的变化）
Indicates a significant alteration or shift from a previous state. Used to describe important transformations.
- The invention of the internet brought about a big change in communication.
- Her promotion was a big change in her career path.

**live in Dubai** （住在迪拜）
Describes the act of residing in the city of Dubai. A simple and direct statement of residence.
- Many expatriates live in Dubai for work opportunities.
- She decided to live in Dubai for a few years.

**big dreams** （宏伟的梦想）
Refers to ambitious aspirations and hopes for the future. Often used in motivational or aspirational contexts.
- Dubai is a city that attracts people with big dreams.
- He moved to the city to pursue his big dreams.
