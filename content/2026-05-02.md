## 🔗 表达 / Chunking Expressions

**power plant** （发电厂）
A place where electricity is made, usually from burning fuel or using nuclear energy. This is a common and important term in news and general conversation.
- The new wind power plant will provide clean energy for the city.
- Many people lost their jobs when the old power plant closed down.

**clean the area** （清理该区域）
To remove dirt, pollution, or dangerous substances from a specific location. It implies a process of making a place safe and tidy again.
- After the festival, volunteers helped to clean the area.
- The cleanup crew worked for weeks to clean the area after the oil spill.

**is still happening** （仍在发生）
Used to describe an event or situation that has not ended and continues in the present. It emphasizes the ongoing nature of something.
- The peace talks are still happening, but there is little progress.
- The search for the missing hikers is still happening despite bad weather.

**wants everyone to be safe** （希望每个人都安全）
Expresses a strong desire for the well-being and security of all people. It's a common sentiment in political and social contexts.
- The government says it wants everyone to be safe during the holidays.
- As a parent, I just want everyone to be safe when they play outside.
