## 🔗 表达 / Chunking Expressions

**chart-topping songs** （排行榜歌曲）
Songs that are at the very top of music charts, meaning they are the most popular and widely played at a given time. It's a common phrase in the music industry.
- Beyoncé released several chart-topping songs this year.
- This artist is known for their chart-topping songs that stay popular for months.

**hidden details** （隐藏的细节）
Specific pieces of information that are not obvious or easily found, often requiring deeper research or knowledge. Used for interesting facts.
- The documentary revealed hidden details about the actor's early life.
- Fans love finding hidden details in their favorite video games.

**keeps the audience engaged** （使观众保持参与）
To make people interested and involved in something, so they don't get bored. Often used for entertainment or educational formats.
- The interactive museum exhibit keeps the audience engaged with hands-on activities.
- A good storyteller knows how to keep the audience engaged from beginning to end.

**ending the quiz on a high note** （在亮点中结束测验）
To finish something in a very successful or positive way. It suggests a strong and memorable conclusion.
- The band played their biggest hit as an encore, ending the concert on a high note.
- She got a promotion just before retiring, ending her career on a high note.
