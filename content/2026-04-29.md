To criticize someone or say negative things about them. Often used informally.

To present or display a video for others to watch. Common in media and online contexts.

To create or produce something, or to cause something to happen. The meaning depends on context.

To appear in a negative or unfavorable way, or to be associated with something negative. Often used when trying to protect reputation.

A warning to be cautious or vigilant during a specific situation or activity. Used to encourage awareness and prevent mistakes or danger.
