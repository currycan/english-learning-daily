## 📖 文章 / Article

04-05-2026 07:00

For a long time, Sweden used many computers in schools. Every student had a tablet. But now, the government is changing the rules.

The government wants to use paper books and pens again. They think that children cannot read well because they look at screens all day. They want children to write by hand to learn better. There are new rules already today. Small preschool children don’t use tablets in school anymore. Soon, students will not be allowed to use mobile phones in class. Some studies say that screens are bad for young brains. Experts say that screens distract children. They don’t look at the teacher but at the screen.

Other countries in Europe are considering doing the same thing. Some people say this is a mistake because the future is digital. But Sweden thinks that books are better for young students.

Difficult words: preschool ( children who are too young for regular school), allowed (when you can do something), distract (to take your mind away from what you are doing).

## 🔗 表达 / Chunking Expressions

**changing the rules** （改变规则）
This phrase means to alter or update the existing regulations or policies. It's a common phrase used in formal and informal contexts when discussing updates to laws or guidelines.
- The company is changing the rules about working from home.
- The school board decided on changing the rules for student attendance.

**write by hand** （手写）
To produce written text using a pen or pencil, as opposed to typing on a keyboard. This is often used when emphasizing the physical act of writing.
- In the past, students had to write by hand for all their exams.
- She prefers to write by hand in her journal.

**bad for young brains** （对年轻的大脑不好）
This phrase refers to something that has a negative impact on the development or health of the brains of children or adolescents. It's often used in discussions about health and education.
- Many parents worry that too much screen time is bad for young brains.
- Scientists are researching if certain foods are bad for young brains.

**digital future** （数字化的未来）
This refers to a future where technology and the internet play a central role in most aspects of life, including work, communication, and education. It's a common term in discussions about technological progress.
- Some believe that preparing students for the digital future means more computer use.
- The company is investing heavily in the digital future of its services.
