A federal jury in New York found that Live Nation and its subsidiary Ticketmaster have been operating as an illegal monopoly.

The court concluded that the entertainment giant stifled competition, which led to high ticket prices and poor service for fans.

Although the US Department of Justice settled, a coalition of 27 states continued the lawsuit and sought a structural breakup of the company. Experts argue that separating Ticketmaster from Live Nation is necessary to bring fairness back to the industry. Furthermore, there are calls to limit Live Nation’s control over artist management and concert venues.

While Live Nation plans to appeal, many believe this verdict marks the beginning of the end for their total dominance of the live entertainment market.

## 🔗 表达 / Chunking Expressions

**operating as an illegal monopoly** （作为非法垄断经营）
This phrase describes a company that controls a market unfairly, with no competition.
- The company was accused of operating as an illegal monopoly in the software industry.
- Many small businesses struggle when large corporations operate as an illegal monopoly.

**stifled competition** （扼杀了竞争）
This means that a company prevented other businesses from competing fairly, often by using its power.
- The new regulations were designed to prevent large companies from stifling competition.
- By buying up all the suppliers, the company effectively stifled competition.

**breakup of the company** （拆分公司）
This refers to the legal process of dividing a large company into smaller, separate businesses.
- Antitrust laws sometimes lead to the breakup of the company.
- The government is considering a breakup of the company to encourage more competition.

**marks the beginning of the end** （标志着...的终结的开始）
This idiomatic expression means that a particular event or development is the first sign that something, usually something powerful or established, is going to fail or stop existing.
- The introduction of smartphones marked the beginning of the end for basic mobile phones.
- For some people, the rising cost of living marks the beginning of the end for their dreams of homeownership.
